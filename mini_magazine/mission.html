Mission

Amish This site will inform about a Amish community who lives in Lancaster county in Pennsylvania and Adam county in Indiana. When people visit Lancaster county or Adam county, you will see Amish people who are dressed in distinct way. Most of the men have beard and wear hats. Men wear suspendars. Ladies wear bonnet and long dresses. You will find most of Amish travel by buggies.

Audience:

Audience going to be whoever is curious about Amish people, why they dress like this, why they travel by buggies, why they don't believe in electricity.

Mission

My mission to inform to my audience about Amish culture and why they are dressed in a certain way and what is their religion and other aspects of their culture that perk your curiosity.
