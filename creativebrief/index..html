This mini magazine website will explore Lancaster Amish community and their lifestyles. It will explains you where they come from and what are their customs. It will explain about the reasons behind their simple life

Lancaster Amish

Overview and Objective:

Purpose of this mini magazine website to revealing interesting facts about Amish people live in Lancaster county in Pennsylvania. A recent statistic puts the population of the Amish at 250,000 in the United States and Canada with an estimated 20 million tourists a year going out of their way to catch glimpses of these people who insist on clinging to their “Old World Ways”.

Target Audience:

People who are curious about the peculiar people and their lifestyles. What do they teach about their simplistic lifestyles.

Mission:

Teach readers about Lancaster Amish way of life.
